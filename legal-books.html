Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) 2023

The new criminal code that replaces the Indian Penal Code

Chapter I - General

Section 1: Title and extent of operation of the Code

This Sanhita may be called the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023. Every person shall be liable to punishment under this Sanhita and not otherwise for every act or omission contrary to the provisions thereof, of which he shall be guilty within India.

Section 2: Punishment of offences committed within India

Every person shall be liable to punishment under this Sanhita and not otherwise for every act or omission contrary to the provisions thereof, of which he shall be guilty within India.

Chapter II - General Explanations

Section 3: General explanations

In this Sanhita, every definition of an offence, every penal provision, and every illustration of every such definition or penal provision, shall be understood subject to the exceptions contained in the Chapter entitled "General Exceptions".

The new evidence act replacing the Indian Evidence Act

Section 1: Short title, extent and commencement

This Adhiniyam may be called the Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam, 2023. It extends to the whole of India and applies to all judicial proceedings in or before any Court.

Chapter II - Of the Relevancy of Facts

Section 3: Evidence may be given of facts in issue and relevant facts

Evidence may be given in any suit or proceeding of the existence or non-existence of every fact in issue and of such other facts as are hereinafter declared to be relevant, and of no others.
